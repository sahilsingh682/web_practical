PROCESS

Our Process

Our design process follows approach. We begin with a deep understanding of your neeeds and
create a planning template.

1

Requirement Gathering

The collecting of requirements is an essential first step in the software development
process. It entails mehodically gathering and examining data from project teams,
stakeholders,and end users to comprehend their requirements and expectations.
The scope, goals,and restrictions of the project are defined as the result of this
process, which lays the groundwork for a successful project excution.

2

Prototype

A product,system,software,application prototype is an early version or mockup
of the actual thing. it acts as a concrete illustration of the design concept,enabling
stakeholders to picture and engage with the features and functionalities of the
product.Before development,protyping aids in spotting potential problems,
gathering input,and improving the finished product.



3

Deployment



The finished programme or system is related and made accessible to end users
during the deployment step of the software development lifecycle.On production
servers or server or devices,software must be installed,set up,and activated . A smooth
transition from development to actual operation is ensured by proper deployment.
To guarantee the best performance and reliability,testing,monitoring,and
maintanance are essential components of this phase.

4

Support & Maintanance

Sotware development is followed by a continous process called support &
maintanance. It entails helping and users, resolving problems, and taking care of
bugs or upgrades. The system is kept up to date with changing requirements and
technical improvements through routine maintanance operations like software
upgrades,secuirty patches,and performance enhancements. A long-lasting and
dependable software solution is ensured by effective support and maintanance.
